Breaking into Tech

A community of programmers

Overview

The tech industry is thriving. A number of tech companies are embracing creativity and hiring individuals with a passion and a quest for how to break into the tech industry. Working in a tech company allows you to explore various career dimensions and hone your interpersonal skills. As technology evolves exponentially and has a rising impact on economies, there seems to be no better time to get into tech than now. However, apart from all the appeal, how difficult is the transition from a non-technology profession into tech? Many people believe that they need a relevant degree to break into tech, which fortunately is not true. Computer science and technology is an industry based on innovation and skills – not on academic prowess. Tech jobs require people with diverse skills and intelligent, fast-learning minds. 66% of the successful programmers are self-trained with no formal tech education. Therefore, with determination and the right planning, here is how you can jump-start your career in tech.
